Ramen


Description

Very nice taste of noodle with Japanese seasoning

Ingredients

  • noodle
  • dry seasoning
  • onion oil

Steps

  1. Boil 500 ml water in a pot
  2. Put noodle in the boiling water
  3. Put dry seasoning and onion oil in a bowl
  4. Once noodle cooked, put in the bowl. stir with the seasoning and oil.
  5. Ramen is done, eat while warm.
